# Privacy Policy

At TikTok Calculator, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any information you may provide while using our service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our TikTok earnings calculator tool.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **TikTok Usernames**: When you use our calculator, you provide TikTok usernames for analysis
- **Usage Preferences**: Language preferences and theme settings stored locally on your device

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymous data about how you interact with our website
- **Technical Information**: Browser type, device information, and IP address for security and performance
- **Cookies**: Essential cookies for website functionality and user preferences

## What We DON'T Collect

We want to be clear about what we do NOT collect:
- Personal identifying information (name, email, phone, address)
- Account passwords or login credentials
- Personal TikTok account access
- Payment or financial information
- Location data beyond general geographic region
- Personal content or private messages

## How We Use Your Information

### TikTok Username Analysis
- Usernames are sent to third-party APIs to retrieve public TikTok data
- No usernames are permanently stored on our servers
- Data is processed immediately and discarded after calculation

### Website Improvement
- Anonymous usage statistics help us improve our service
- Technical data helps us optimize performance and fix bugs
- User preferences enhance your experience across visits

### Legal Compliance
- We may process data to comply with legal obligations
- Protect against fraud, abuse, or security threats
- Enforce our Terms of Service

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

### Third-Party APIs
- We use external services to fetch public TikTok data
- These services have their own privacy policies
- No personal information is shared beyond the username being analyzed

### Analytics Services
- Google Analytics (anonymous usage data)
- Vercel Analytics (performance metrics)
- These services help us understand website usage patterns

### No Sale of Data
- We do not sell, rent, or trade your information
- We do not share personal information with advertisers
- Your data is never used for marketing purposes

## Data Security

### Protection Measures
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- No permanent storage of user queries
- Regular security updates and monitoring
- Access controls and security protocols

### Data Retention
- TikTok usernames: Not stored permanently
- Usage analytics: Anonymized and aggregated
- Technical logs: Retained for security purposes (30 days maximum)

## Your Rights and Choices

### User Control
- No account creation required
- Anonymous usage of all features
- Control over cookies and local storage through browser settings
- Ability to clear stored preferences at any time

### Opt-Out Options
- Disable analytics cookies through browser settings
- Use private/incognito browsing mode
- Block third-party cookies if preferred

## Children's Privacy

TikTok Calculator does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

## International Users

Our service is available globally. By using TikTok Calculator, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy, regardless of your location.
