# About TikTok Calculator

Our platform provides instant insights into TikTok account metrics including follower engagement, video performance, and revenue potential.

## 🔍 Understanding the Metrics

### Estimated Earnings
Our earnings calculator uses industry-standard rates based on:
- Follower count and engagement levels
- Video views and interaction rates
- Market averages for TikTok monetization
- Brand partnership typical rates

### Engagement Rate
Calculated as: (Total Hearts ÷ (Followers × Videos)) × 100
This metric helps assess how actively engaged an audience is with the content.

### Earnings Per Video
Average estimated revenue per video: Total Earnings ÷ Number of Videos
Useful for understanding content value and planning strategies.
